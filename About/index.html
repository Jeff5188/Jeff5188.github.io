About

刘展弘，2009年9月18日出生于中国大陆上海市，现就读于上海民办包玉刚实验学校，曾参加过美高数学竞赛（AMC8获得世界前5%）、美国奥林匹克编程竞赛（USACO，现为金级选手）、美国计算机竞赛（ACSL，获得全球二等奖）、入选霍普金斯天才营并获得优胜奖。此外，他还通过了中国社会艺术水平长笛6级考试。他兴趣爱好广泛，热爱体育运动，如篮球、棍网球、游泳等。2022年开始参加上海科学社组织的科研项目培训，在导师的指导下进行智能小车的科学研究。这是他第一次参加科研项目，遇到了许多的困难，比如疫情封控、设备短缺、主要学习复杂的专业知识并熟练运用等。虽然存在各种困难，但经过不懈的努力，和导师一起攻克了这个项目。在项目结束后，他对比曾经发现自己不仅在专业知识方面收获颇丰，还建立了不畏艰辛、勇于挑战的信心，这为他未来面对更大的挑战积累了宝贵的经验。

Zhanhong Liu, born on September 18, 2009 in Shanghai, Mainland China, is currently studying at the private YK Pao Experimental School in Shanghai. He has participated in the American Advanced Placement Math Competition (AMC8 to obtain the top 5% in the world), the U.S. Olympic Programming Competition (USACO, now a gold-level competitor), the U.S. Computer Competition (ACSL to obtain the second prize worldwide), and was selected for the John Hopkins Camp for the Gifted and Talented and won the merit award. In addition, he passed the Chinese Social Arts Proficiency Flute Level 6 exam. He has a wide range of interests and loves sports, such as basketball, stick tennis, swimming, etc. In 2022, he started to participate in a scientific research project training organized by the Shanghai Science Club, where he conducted scientific research on smart cars under the guidance of his mentor. It was his first time to participate in a scientific research project, and he encountered many difficulties: including epidemic sealing and control, shortage of equipment, and mainly learning complex expertise and applying it proficiently. Though faced with all the difficulties, he overcame the challenges with his mentor after disdainful efforts. At the end of the project, he discovered that he not only gained a lot in terms of professional knowledge, but has also built up the confidence to defy hardships and challenges, which has accumulated valuable experience for him to tackle bigger challenges in the future.
