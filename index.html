Community And Volunteer

2023-10-02

topic video

During the summer after seventh grade, I had the incredible opportunity to volunteer at an English camp for middle school students in Guangxi Province, China. Guangxi is one of the less economically developed provinces in China, and many students there do not have the same access to English language education as students in bigger cities. For two years straight, I persisted to going to this camp and taught children in poverty ESL.

Teaching Image School Image

Our goal at the camp was not just to improve the students’ English skills through memorization and drills, but also to foster their love and enthusiasm for learning the language. I designed interactive lessons and activities to keep the students engaged and having fun while practicing English. One of the most popular activities was playing games like Hangman and crossword puzzles. Whenever we broke out the games, the energy in the classroom would instantly lift as the students began competing in small groups to solve the puzzles or guess the words before their classmates. You could see the smiles and laughs as they worked together in English to solve the puzzles and mysteries.

Certificate Certificate

The gratitude and enthusiasm from the students was extremely rewarding. At the closing ceremony, many of them expressed how much fun they had learning English in a relaxed, playful environment. Witnessing their progress and love of learning grow over just one week was incredibly inspiring. I’m thankful for the opportunity I had to volunteer and hope to continue finding ways to support education in underserved communities through fun and creative teaching.

STEM

2023-10-02

topic video

USACO score USACO account

Since I was young, I’ve been really into robotics, which is what sparked my interest in engineering and computer science. I started off by diving deep into robotics, taking part in several prestigious competitions that allowed me to apply my knowledge into action. I attended the John Hopkins Game design course and finished my final project and getting the CTY high honors. In middle school, I competed in the ACSL math competition. This offered me some valuable experience and made me even more interested as I had hands-on experiences with design, electronics, and programming. As my interest continued to grow, I joined the VEX Robotics Competition that is a part of the CREATE US Open robotics tournament. Being the team captain in this competition allowed me to practice my leadership skills as so I led strategic design decisions, solved mechanical issues, and refined the software. Eventually, our hard work paid off when our robot placed 2nd regionally. Throughout the school years, I also attended seminars and courses at the Shanghai Science Association to further enrich my robotics skills. One highlight of my robotics journey was when I wrote a research paper on optimizing the algorithms for intelligent car navigation. I explored adjusting the proportional–integral–derivative controller to enable smoother turning and less accidents. My paper was well-received by professors, validating my growth as a robotics engineer. Through a combination of competitions, courses, and research, I have developed advanced skills and a firm passion for robotics, making me eager to apply my knowledge in high school and beyond.

topic video

It was an honor to represent Yk Pao school School at the prestigious Shanghai Maker Fair. As a member of the school’s robotics club, I saw it as an opportunity to showcase my intelligent car project.
Over the past year, I designed and programmed an autonomous robot that worked through my improved A* algorithm. I implemented sensors and coding to allow it to navigate independently.
At the Maker Fair, I demonstrated our robot’s abilities to crowds. I especially enjoyed interacting with the children and sparking their curiosity about STEM. It was very rewarding to see the interest we generated and to represent all that Yk Pao students can achieve through hands-on learning and innovation. The event was a wonderful platform to share our passion and inspire others.

Music

topic video

Concert Images Concert Images

Music captured me at a young age. Playing the Piano and participating in my elementary school choir ignited my passion for music. However, after two whole years, I found my true calling – flute. The first time I held that gleaming instrument, I was smitten. I practiced diligently for four years, progressing through the levels to reach the advanced sixth tier. The pinnacle came when I was elected to be first chair in the school band. After honing my craft for years, I now share my love of music with my talented peers. As our melodies intertwine delicately during our performance, I knew I found my niece. Music is my passion and flute is my voice.

Sports

2023-10-02

topic video

Sports has been a major part of my childhood as I found joy in teamwork and the excitement in competing. Activities such as swimming, surfing, basketball, and snowboarding boosted my confidence, taught me discipline and perseverance, and promoted lasting friendships.

I was first interested in taekwondo in elementary school where I practiced for four whole years. My hard work eventually paid off in my final year as I earned my first first-place medal, marking a proud achievement in my athletic journey.

Training Images Training Images

However, it was lacrosse that took center stage in my competitive journey when I joined the Shanghai Lynx U15 lacrosse team in 2021. After months of grueling practices perfecting my cradling, scooping, and shooting skills, I made the starting lineup. For two years, we trained intensely as a team - both with our coaches and on our own – which solidified our bonds. I then became part of the main club team, enduring all weather conditions during exhausting drills and scrimmages.

I forged strong bonds of trust with my teammates while preparing to bring down the trophy at the 2023 national championship. On competition day, victory followed victory, leading us to the finals. We left everything on the field, fought for every ground ball, and capitalized every scoring chance. Finally, a whistle signaled our victory. We celebrated the culmination of our blood, sweat, and tears as we were proud to represent Shanghai on the national stage.
